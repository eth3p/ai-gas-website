## 🎯 Traffic Acquisition Strategy

### Phase 1: Foundation (Month 1-2)
**Goal**: 500 daily visitors

#### Tactics:
1. **SEO Optimization**
   - Target long-tail keywords
   - Optimize all articles for SEO
   - Build internal linking structure

2. **Social Media**
   - Twitter/X: Daily AI news and tips
   - LinkedIn: Professional AI insights
   - Reddit: Share valuable content in relevant subreddits

3. **Content Marketing**
   - Publish 2-3 articles per week
   - Create comprehensive guides
   - Update existing content regularly

### Phase 2: Growth (Month 3-6)
**Goal**: 2000 daily visitors

#### Tactics:
1. **Guest Posting**
   - Write for other AI/tech blogs
   - Get backlinks from authority sites
   - Expand audience reach

2. **Email List Building**
   - Offer AI tools checklist as lead magnet
   - Weekly newsletter with valuable insights
   - Promote affiliate products to subscribers

3. **Community Building**
   - Create Discord community
   - Host webinars and Q&A sessions
   - Engage with readers on social media

### Phase 3: Scaling (Month 7-12)
**Goal**: 5000+ daily visitors

#### Tactics:
1. **Partnerships**
   - Collaborate with AI tool companies
   - Joint webinars and promotions
   - Cross-promotion with similar sites

2. **Paid Advertising** (once profitable)
   - Google Ads for high-intent keywords
   - Social media ads for content promotion
   - Retargeting campaigns

3. **Product Development**
   - Create premium AI tool guides
   - Develop online courses
   - Launch paid membership community
